\section{Question:}
What is the result of \( 2 + 3 \)?
\section{Answer:}
Breaking down the recursion step-by-step:
\[
    \begin{aligned}
        2 + 3 & = (2 + 2) + 1             \\
              & = ((2 + 1) + 1) + 1       \\
              & = (((2 + 0) + 1) + 1) + 1 \\
              & = ((2 + 1) + 1) + 1       \\
              & = (3 + 1) + 1             \\
              & = 4 + 1                   \\
              & = 5
    \end{aligned}
\]
