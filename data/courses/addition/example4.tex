\section{Example 4: \( 5 + 2 \)}
Applying the recursive steps:
\[
    \begin{aligned}
        5 + 2 & = (5 + 1) + 1       \\
              & = ((5 + 0) + 1) + 1 \\
              & = (5 + 1) + 1       \\
              & = 6 + 1             \\
              & = 7
    \end{aligned}
\]
